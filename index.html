About Me

Meet the person behind the blog

  • Name : Ankan Poddar
  • Age :
  • Hometown : Sodepur, West Bengal
  • Studying At : NIT Durgapur

Since you're here, you realize that a self-obsessed guy is going to talk about himself. Let me do that. My journey started from a small town in Bihar, and hence I had no exposure to programming and computer science whatsoever. But I had an interest, and a computer, and yeah, also a slow internet connection which helped me learn a lot about computers, not programming though.

Then I came to West Bengal for higher studies and I joined a course with computer science as my fifth subject. Frankly, I was scared to see that all my peers had done quite a bit of programming and I was a complete zero. But I had the will to learn and I have learnt a lot over those two years. I joined a Computer Science and Engineering course in National Institute of Technology, Durgapur. Once I was here, I was overwhelmed to see how many people approached me to teach them programming just so they could pass the exams.

All this has led me to start this blog. This blog is for them who think that C is very tough to learn. I started this blog so that I can help beginners to learn C and help those who already know about C to strengthen their grasp and I also learn a lot in the process. I wish everyone will have a good time learning about C here. Cheers.
